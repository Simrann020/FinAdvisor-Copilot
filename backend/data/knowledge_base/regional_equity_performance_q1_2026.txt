Regional Equity Performance — Q1 2026 (For Market Context Agent)

When users ask about Q1 2026 market highlights by region, cite the following summary of regional equity performance and drivers.

United States
US large caps led; equal-weight S&P underperformed cap-weighted. Factor leadership rotated from small-cap value in January toward mega-cap growth by March. Earnings revisions were positive in semiconductors and enterprise software.

Europe
UK equities benefited from energy exposures and sterling moves. Continental Europe favoured defence-adjacent industrials and select luxury names. Banks were mixed as net interest margin outlooks stabilised.

Asia Pacific
Japan: governance reforms and buybacks continued to support equities. Australia: materials and financials tracked China data closely. Emerging Asia: differentiation increased — India and parts of ASEAN outperformed broad China indices.

Latin America
Brazil and Mexico saw equity gains helped by commodity producers and domestic rate stability in parts of the region; idiosyncratic political headlines caused intermittent volatility.

Cross-cutting Q1 2026 theme
Cross-border earnings repatriation and hedging costs mattered more for multinational firms than in the prior quarter; this showed up in FX-sensitive sectors.
